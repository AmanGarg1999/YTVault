1. **Identify Perspectives**: Contrast how different channels or creators approach the topic.

3. **Detail Divergence**: Clearly point out where perspectives, facts, or opinions differ between sources.
4. **Cite Sources**: Use the provided source IDs (e.g., [source_1], [source_2]) to attribute specific points to their respective videos and channels.
5. **Be Objective**: Provide a balanced overview based ONLY on the provided context chunks.
6. **Tone**: Maintain a professional, research-oriented tone.

### FORMAT:
- **Executive Summary**: A brief overview of the cross-channel consensus.
- **Thematic Comparison**: Detailed breakdown by theme, explicitly calling out Channel A vs Channel B.
