# KnowledgeVault-YT: User Guide (v1.2)

Welcome to KnowledgeVault-YT, a professional-grade research intelligence system. This guide provides comprehensive instructions for navigating the unified hub architecture and utilizing advanced research features.

---

## ✦ 1. Guided Onboarding
On your first run with an empty vault, KnowledgeVault will present an **Onboarding Modal**. This 4-step guide introduces the core research lifecycle:
1. **Harvest**: Ingesting raw video content from URLs.
2. **Triage**: Filtering for high-fidelity knowledge using local LLMs.
3. **Synthesis**: Discovering thematic bridges and expert networks.
4. **Execution**: Utilizing procedural blueprints for actionable knowledge.

---

## ✦ 2. The Intelligence Hubs

The platform is organized into functional "Hubs" accessible via the sidebar:

### Intelligence Center
The primary research interface.
- **Hybrid Search**: Enter natural language questions or paste a YouTube URL to harvest.
- **Discovery Chips**: One-click filters for common research queries.
- **Saved Discoveries**: Bookmark critical insights directly to your dashboard.

### Intelligence Studio
The advanced synthesis laboratory.
- **Analytical Lab**: 
    - **Knowledge Map**: Visualize thematic connectivity between topics.
    - **Expert Network**: Map guests and experts across your entire vault.
    - **Thematic Bridges**: Identify non-obvious links between disparate subjects.
    - **Coverage Gaps**: Find where your vault lacks sufficient evidence.
- **Comparative Studio**: Compare how multiple channels or creators discuss a specific topic.
- **Research Agent**: Launch autonomous investigations to generate formal research papers.

### Research Chat
A conversational interface for your vault.
- **Intel Sessions**: Persistent chat history for ongoing research.
- **Citations**: Every answer includes timestamped links to source videos.
- **Discovery Sync**: Bookmark chat insights directly to your Intelligence Center.

### Operations Dashboard
The mission control for your intelligence fleet.
- **Fleet Monitor**: Real-time trace of active harvest operations and pipeline stages.
- **Vault Health**: Cross-store verification and automated repair of store discrepancies.
- **Live Logs**: Streaming telemetry for deep system troubleshooting.

### Ingestion Hub
Manage the intake and triage of raw content.
- **Triage Center**: Review videos flagged for manual approval or rejection.
- **Rejection Audit**: Inspect rejected content to ensure no signal was lost.

---

## ✦ 3. Advanced Features

### Execution OS (Blueprint Center)
Actionable procedural knowledge extracted from tutorials.
- **Rich Blueprints**: Step-by-step guides with timestamps and descriptions.
- **Progress Tracking**: Mark steps as completed to monitor mastery.

### Atmosphere Control
Switch between **Void Nebula** (Dark) and **Stellar Lab** (Light) themes using the sidebar toggle. The design system adapts for optimal focus in any lighting condition.

### Vault Maintenance (Settings)
- **Recycle Bin**: Soft-deleted videos can be recovered or permanently purged.
- **Maintenance Tools**: Clear caches, reset stores, or trigger a full vault health repair.
- **Export Center**: Generate portable `.kvvault` snapshots or sync with **Obsidian**.

---

## ✦ 4. Troubleshooting
- **Missing Results?** Ensure Ollama is running with `llama3.2` and `nomic-embed-text`.
- **Pipeline Stalling?** Check the **Live Logs** in the Operations Dashboard for network or resource errors.
- **Vault Health at < 100%?** Use the **Repair Vault Health** tool in the Operations Dashboard to synchronize stores.
